Mechanical Engineering Formulas

Stress and Strain

  • Stress (σ): \( \sigma = \frac{F}{A} \)
  • Strain (ε): \( \epsilon = \frac{\Delta L}{L} \)
  • Young's Modulus (E): \( E = \frac{\sigma}{\epsilon} \)

Beam Deflection

  • Deflection (δ) for Cantilever Beam: \( \delta = \frac{FL^3}{3EI} \)
  • Deflection (δ) for Simply Supported Beam: \( \delta = \frac{5wL^4}{384EI} \)

Fluid Mechanics

  • Reynolds Number (Re): \( Re = \frac{\rho v D}{\mu} \)
  • Bernoulli's Equation: \( P_1 + \frac{1}{2} \rho v_1^2 + \rho g h_1 = P_2 + \frac{1}{2} \rho v_2^2 + \rho g h_2 \)

Thermodynamics

  • Heat Transfer (Q): \( Q = m c \Delta T \)
  • First Law of Thermodynamics: \( \Delta U = Q - W \)
